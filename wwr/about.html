Zambezi Rafting Adventures

At Zambezi Rafting Adventures, our mission is to provide safe, thrilling, and unforgettable rafting experiences for adventurers of all skill levels. We believe in fostering a deep appreciation for nature while delivering the ultimate adrenaline rush through carefully guided expeditions.

History

Founded in 1995 by outdoor enthusiasts, Rafting Adventures began as a small family operation with just two rafts. Over the past three decades, we've grown into one of the most trusted rafting companies in the region, serving over 50,000 satisfied customers while maintaining our commitment to safety, environmental stewardship, and exceptional customer service.
